ERP CRM SOLUTIONS

CRM systems store data. As the name goes, Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems mainly record and store those pieces of information that deal with customer interactions. The data is standardized and easily sharable for a fluent use. Such storage of information is useful at every stage by the marketing and sales departments in the organization. CRM systems come with all the necessary features to store and access the data at all times. They can be used by marketing departments to analyse and even automate marketing campaigns, by sales departments to record any important sales transactions, and to provide better customer support based on the data recorded. ERM software streamlines business operations. ERM can be implemented and used by multiple departments throughout the organization. It has the feature of interconnected management of specific business purposes that allows every department get the access to information in real tie. Tracking manufacture and supply chains, managing benefits, payroll and employee information are some of the many features of ERM.

VIDEO CONFERENCING

Video conferencing gained popularity in recent years due to a number of factors. It is one of those technologies that brought significant changes – involving and beyond the corporate world. Such type of a software allows users to hold face-to-face meetings over the internet, from different locations. It proves to be convenient when businesses have offices in varied locations or when there is a need to hold critically urgent meetings. Since business travel is often a costly and hassle-based option, users now prefer holding meetings via video conferencing platforms. A common downside to these platforms would be data connectivity problems that sometimes act as a hurdle. To enhance the experience, some corporations have dedicated conferencing rooms with high quality cameras and microphone devices. Video conferencing apps can also act like a traditional classroom to benefit students who are taking distant education. Furnished with a variety of features, such software applications can be used on a variety of devices and allow the users to have the necessary control over meetings according to the nature of uses.

GRAPHIC DESIGNING

Graphic designing is an integration of symbols, images, texts, etc. to form a visual representation that is helps in illustrating and communicating visually. Main areas where graphic designing is used commonly are corporate logos/branding, advertisements, newspapers, magazines, books, web designing, product packaging, and more. Other than these, road signs and manuals also portray the creativity of graphic designing. An appealing, creative and relevant design can become a tool that boosts the sale of goods and services. Not so surprisingly, graphics designing is also applied and put into practice by the entertainment industry for the purpose of story-telling. Graphic designing typically involves optimization and better presentation of the existing content. It’s not just the eye-pleasing factor that makes a difference. A meaningful concept always enhances the designs. Graphic designing and photography also go hand-in-hand for special purposes. Designers hire photographers or illustrators to create original pieces of content relatable to their needs. Graphic designing can be done with the help of computer software or simply by hand.

CLOUD COMPUTING

Cloud computing is the on-demand delivery of IT resources over the Internet with pay-as-you-go pricing. Instead of buying, owning, and maintaining physical data centers and servers, you can access technology services, such as computing power, storage, and databases, on an as-needed basis from a cloud provider like Amazon Web Services (AWS).
